Interjections

Interjections show big feelings in tiny words. Words like "Wow!", "Oops!", or "Yay!" tell us exactly how someone feels super fast!

They often come with exclamation points (!) because they're just that excited to be there! Interjections can stand alone or start a sentence.

They're independent little words! We use them all the time without even thinking about it. Next time you're surprised or happy, listen to what you say—it might be an interjection!
